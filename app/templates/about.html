A Few Words About Us

We Voluntary Help for Almost 0 Years

Aaeena is an India-based crowdfunding platform dedicated to help the ones in need and empowering them through the help from the community across the nation. With the goal to make a change in the society, a group of young entrepreneurs established this online platform in 2020. As the entire world was in distress be it economy, healthcare, social services, or personal aspects, Aaeena was formed to reach out to people and help them through a model of person to person giving.

Crowdfunding is emerging as a viable and potent solution to provide different kinds of help or assistance to people, however, the intermediary charges often makes it complicated. We, at Aaeena, are dedicated to make sure that those complications are dealt with properly and every single penny goes to the betterment of the mankind. Being a blooming organisation, Aaeena aims to go a long way and bring changes to lives of people and the society. Aaeena is the potent reflection of what assistance and solidarity can do at tough times.

Mission: Title Here

Easing the model of person to person giving to help people who are in need. In this way, we aim to alleviate poverty, provide healthcare assistance to others, and ensure help for people in several other ways. Aaeena is an online crowdfunding platform which aims to encourage people to provide financial help for social and humanitarian causes with ease through a reliable agency. Our mission is to strengthen the community in the nation and collectively bring smile to the faces of people who are in need.

Vission

Continue providing help and assistance to people for different causes by encouraging the community and spreading awareness. Tough times will continue to exist and to be faced (at personal and community levels) and therefore, we will continue to battle those situations with solidarity. Aaeena is a blooming organisation and by expanding our network in the next 5years through voluntary integration, we hope to become a reliable nation-wide crowdsourcing platform contributing to at least 30% of the nation-wide crowdsourced funds.
